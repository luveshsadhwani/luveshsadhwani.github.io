About Me

A chemical engineering graduate, wandering programmer Luvesh has developed a structured approach to solving complex problems. Starting out with excel formulas at the early age of 10, Luvesh has always been driven to utilise the power of programming to solve logistical problems.

As a technical support executive in SOLS Energy (Solar Panel Installation Services), Luvesh initiated, planned and executed a monitoring system that sends automated alerts via email to the technical team for them to detect potential issues or malfunctions. Luvesh used Google Apps Scripts to set up this system.

Currently, Luvesh is working on building static websites and aims to become a full-stack web developer in the future. His ideal career would consist of working in-house in a software development team to optimise internal business operations.

As a wanderer even in his leisure time, Luvesh takes a keen interest in reading, politics, video games and renewable energy.
